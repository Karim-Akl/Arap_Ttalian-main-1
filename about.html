---- who we are ----

Arab Italian Company is an international freight forwarding agency, specialized in cargo and transport and is represented across the globe by our appointed branches and agents. Our agency works hard to fulfill our main target, to manage the business efficiently, and to provide our clients with highest services at lowest prices through highly trained staff who are capable of handling any type of cargo movement both Import & Export, Ocean & Air . Arab Italian Company was established as Arabital Egypt in 1999 in partnership with Arabital Italia S.P.A. which was established in 1989 in Milan / Italy . On 2020 After Covid 19 , the whole world faced so many challenges and we have changed our legal entity to be L.L.C company conformed with the Egyptian new law of investment to get benefits from all facilitates introduce by Egyptian government With around 65 Employees we are moving daily shipments between sea , air and land from all over the world . in collaboration with international chain of forwarders we can say now we are covering the major parts of the world . We in Arab Italian believe that the Human Resource is our real investment to improve all new services to our clients to meet their requirements and to satisfy their needs .

In such an era of competition and rapidly fluctuating rate exchanges, we monitor and act accordingly under all circumstances to get over all barriers and problems in the fields of banking, insurance and transportation.

We are members of EIFFA/IATA/FIATA/ WCA / JCtrans . We take pride in offering personalized, professional services in all matters relating to import and export forwarding. Including domestic, trans-border and international freight.

With our strong financial position, a dedicated team experienced in all aspects of international transport, an expanding network of overseas agents, we commitment to deliver on time any time.

Our Mission

ourmission is to be leaders in providing affordable, worry-free and outstanding quality services in freight forwarding to EUROPE, FAR EAST,ASIA,AFRICA, and worldwide. Provides our valued customers with the highest level of service in freight forwarding, custom clearance, and transportation through our own local offices as well as globe network of partners. Through the use of these selected partners, Arab Italian is able to provide a door-to-door forwarding and clearing service at competitive rates to and from most countries worldwide. Relationships with airlines and shipping lines are strong which enables to us negotiate competitive rates, reliable, and efficient services at all times.

Our Vision

to transform Arab Italian Company into a well-established brand that provides one stop solutions to all logistics issues and to be one of the best freight forwarders companies world wide
